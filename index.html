PROFILE SUMMARY

Highly motivated research professional with over eight years of experience spanning telecommunications, information technology, and cybersecurity. By blending extensive industry practice with academic inquiry, I have developed strong analytical skills and a multidimensional perspective on technological innovation. Driven to pursue a Ph.D., I aim to develop and apply advanced artificial intelligence models to tackle complex, unsolved challenges across domains. My approach is inherently interdisciplinary, and I am eager to contribute solutions that address unmet needs in both established and emerging fields.

My research interests include interpretable artificial intelligence, especially deep neural networks, transfer learning, and Kolmogorov-Arnold networks. I am passionate about applying these methodologies to multidisciplinary problems, advancing science and technology for the collective good. Through my work, I seek to enable technological breakthroughs, strengthen digital infrastructure, and contribute meaningfully to national and global progress.
